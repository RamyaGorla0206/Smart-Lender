Automated Evaluation Flow

Our ML pipeline evaluates applications across three systematic dimensions.

1. Demographics

Analyzes marital status, dependents, property region (rural/semi-urban/urban), and educational qualifications to segment applicants correctly.

2. Financial Analysis

Reviews primary applicant incomes, co-applicant financial inputs, requested loan size, and monthly installment terms to verify debt tolerance.

3. Machine Learning Inference

Applies a balanced XGBoost ensemble model trained on thousands of loan profiles to determine approval status and flag high-risk accounts.
